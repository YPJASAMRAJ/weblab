Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences was founded in the year 1986 and it is located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Karunya University offers various UG, PG, Integrated and Research courses in the field of Arts, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Science and Technology. Candidates can apply for the various courses online through the official website of the institute. Admissions are based on first come first serve basis.
